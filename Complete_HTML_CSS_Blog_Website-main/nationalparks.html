National Parks in India- UPSC Notes

In this article, you will learn all about Indian National Parks for UPSC (Environment). Important national parks in India serve as suitable sites for the conservation of natural habitats and animals that provide food. Man-made activities such as development activities, industrial activities, forestry, poaching, hunting and agriculture are prohibited. The boundaries of the national park are clearly defined and no private activities are permitted within the national park.Candidates can also watch the video from the link below.

National Parks:


It is an area designated by the country to preserve the natural environment. National parks have more restrictions than nature reserves. Your boundaries are set and defined. The main purpose of National Parks is to protect the local natural environment and maintain biodiversity.
